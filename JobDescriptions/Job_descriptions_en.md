# Foreman in Meat Production

## Foreman Duties

- **Ongoing contact with the client**  
- **Training new employees** in health and safety, quality and hygiene matters  
- **Analysis of production plans** and information transfer to line leaders  
- **Organization of production line staffing** according to production plan and organizing employee trips to work  
- **Monitoring employee attendance registration**  
- **Coordination of timely production line startup**  
- **Verification and analysis of orders** from the previous day  
- **Control of production processes**  
- **Optimization of employee work pace** on the production line to maximize efficiency  
- **Participation in meetings with the client** and supporting effective cooperation  
- **Selection and training of employees** as machine operators  
- **Analysis of work efficiency** and support of supervision and employees  
- **Completing reports**  
- **Conducting job interviews** with new candidates  

---

## What we can offer you

- **Rate 36.00 PLN gross** per hour (contract of mandate)  
- **Bonuses** for work efficiency and results  
- **Free lunches**  
- **Accommodation**: utility bill payment 400 PLN with free transportation to work  
- **Bonus 1.00 PLN gross** per hour with own accommodation  
- **Bonus 0.40 PLN gross** per hour for own transportation to work  
- **Opportunity for further development** in company structures (to senior Foreman, Coordinator)  
- **Training and work on machines** from leading European manufacturers as well as business trips and work in other facilities  
- **Work 8–12 hours per day**, depending on production needs  
- **Work 6 days per week**  

---

## What is important to us

- **Engagement in work** and the feeling that we can count on you  
- **Knowledge of Polish language** at B1–B2 level  
- **Experience** in a Foreman position or in team management
